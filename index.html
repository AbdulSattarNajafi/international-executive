Run · Build · Protect: AI-Enabled Future

The Sangfor Executive Forum 2026 is an upgraded leadership experience designed for CIOs, IT directors, and security leaders navigating an AI-accelerated era. This year’s theme — Run · Build · Protect: AI-Enabled Future — shows how organizations can keep today’s infrastructure reliable, modernize for what’s next, and protect the business end-to-end, with AI enhancing every stage to make decisions faster and operations more efficient.

The 3 Pillars of AI-Enabled Future

Run

Keep mission‑critical systems stable with consistent operations and predictable performance — so business runs without disruption.

Build

Design a future‑ready foundation for cloud, modern apps, and AI workloads —without over‑engineering or over‑spending.

PROTECT

Outrun threats with AI‑driven security operations, faster detection, and automated response — across the entire environment.

Join us at the Sangfor Executive Forum 2026 to experience the transformative power of AI combined with simplified IT solutions.
